Services

Competitively benchmarked against leading providers, our offer focuses on transparent methods, reproducible code, and regulatory relevance.

PBPK Modelling

Whole‑body PBPK for human and preclinical species; oral, dermal, inhalation; life‑stage & population variability; uncertainty & sensitivity analysis.

  • Dose selection, DDI hypotheses, and reverse dosimetry
  • Mixture PBPK and internal dosimetry for NGRA
  • Regulatory documentation (model plan, V&V summary)

qIVIVE & TK Extrapolation

In vitro potency → in vivo equivalent dose using mechanistic clearance, binding and partitioning; cell and organotypic systems.

  • Free fraction & protein/RBC binding corrections
  • Permeability‑limited tissues and ALI models
  • High‑throughput screening integration

Non‑compartmental & TK Analysis

Cmax, Tmax, AUC, t1/2, CL, V, bioequivalence & BA/BE assessments with robust QC and reporting.

  • Preclinical & clinical datasets (sparse or rich)
  • Automated tables, figures, listings
  • Model‑informed dose justification

Mixture Toxicology & NAMs

Component‑based PBPK, interaction scenarios, and AOP‑informed integration for mixture risk assessment.

  • Scenario analysis & uncertainty propagation
  • Portfolio‑level prioritization
  • Transparent, reproducible workflows

Training & Regulatory Support
